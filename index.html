Relationship Philosophy

A relationship should be like two mountain climbers, who have their own equipment that they maintain themselves. They climb their own mountains (personal goals, friends, hobbies) and then meet around the campfire (sunrise, weekends away) to discuss where they`ve been and what they`ve seen.

Sometimes you climb the mountains together, but you have your own equipment (qualities, strengths, ideas, energy) for the trip not one dragging the other up the mountain, with only one set of equipment.

It is important to do things on our own and not to live in each others pockets, but meet as often as possible to climb that mountain together!
